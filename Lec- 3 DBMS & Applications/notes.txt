DBMS: Database Management System

It is software which allows users to access database, dave data, retrieve it, updata it and manage it

Presence of rules and regulations is important to maintain the data effectively

Applications of DBMS:
Schools and colleges: to store student's records, attendance, staff info, personal details , effectively

banks: to store and maintain customer information, personal details, account numbers and transaction history

airlines: schedule of flights, details of staff, details of passengers etc
